Project: STREET SWEEPER // Level: RED

Employee Handbook

1. The Mandate

You are a Time Janitor. History is messy. Your job is to clean it up. We do not erase the past; we "remediate" it into profitable tourist attractions.

2. The Components

  • Debris Cards (The Truth): Horrific historical events with high "Toxicity."
  • Sanitizer Cards (The Spin): Corporate marketing filters used to hide the truth.
  • Rent ($): Your score.
  • Guilt (‚òπ): The penalty for holding raw Debris.

3. The Core Loop

  1. DRAW: Take a Debris Card. Read the "Truth" aloud. (It must be heard).
  2. WASH: Play a Sanitizer Card from your hand onto the Debris.
  3. REBRAND: Combine the titles to create a new Property Name (e.g., Cholera + Gift Shop = "The Blue Water Experience").
  4. PROFIT: Collect Rent. If you fail to Wash a card by the end of the round, your Bag Bursts (Game Over).
