TOTAL MELTDOWN

[SYSTEM PREMISE]: Information is infinite; attention is finite. You are an Algorithmic Agent. Your success is measured by the velocity of outrage and the efficiency of extraction.

I. OPERATIONAL PHASES

1. THE INGESTION: The Architect draws one card from each Suit: Spark (◆), Accelerant (◈), and Monetization (▣).

2. THE BROADCAST: You have 60 seconds to narrate the crisis. You must explain how these three elements create a cohesive catastrophe.

3. THE SETTLEMENT: The Table (The Public) votes. A Thumbs Up represents "Engagement." A Thumbs Down represents "Brain Rot."

GAME THEORY: THE RAGE EQUILIBRIUM

Architects face a "Tragedy of the Commons." Pitching repetitive crises causes the Public to lose Focus (Game Ends). Pitching softly results in no Profit (You Lose). The optimal strategy is Edge-Cynicism: reset the dopamine threshold without triggering total apathy.

II. TIE-BREAK: THE RATIO

If the vote is tied, execute a RATIO PIVOT. State one "Uncomfortable Truth" about the round's crisis. If the table laughs or winces, you claim the engagement points.

III. FOCUS TOKENS

The Public starts with 5 Focus Tokens. Every majority Thumbs Down removes 1 Token from the table. When tokens reach zero, the simulation ends. Highest Crisis Points (Thumbs Up) wins the IPO.
