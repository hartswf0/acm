The Anti-Crisis Loop

1

Crisis Injection

Draw 1 CRISIS card. Establish base Heat value on the Crisis Track.

2

Vector Application

Attach a VECTOR card to amplify, deflect, or distort. Roll d6 for Virality.

3

Oracle Consultation

Draw 1 ORACLE card. Force a pattern collision: "How does this probe describe the crisis?"

4

Defense Window (10 sec)

Active player must EXPLAIN their intervention. Other players are the JURY. Timer starts immediately.

5

Extraction / Judgment

Jury votes: ACQUIT (+Aura) or CONVICT (-Aura). Winner claims REVENUE or HEAT tokens.

6

Bot Intervention

If Crisis Track > 50, Bot applies Nuance. If Nuance > Heat, game mode shifts.

7

Scroll

All cards slide down one zone. Bottom card is deleted. New round begins.
